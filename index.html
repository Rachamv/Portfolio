About Me

Fullstack Developer | Software Engineer

Hi there! I'm Adewunmi Oladele, also known as Rachamv. I'm a passionate software engineer and data scientist with expertise in full-stack web development, iOS development, and design.

Throughout my career, I have gained experience working on various projects, solving complex problems, and delivering high-quality solutions. I enjoy creating elegant and user-friendly applications that have a positive impact on people's lives.
